The solution

The drill comes home.

One deterministic simulation engine. Three ways to learn how a real dispatcher thinks. Mobile-first, no login, 7 minutes, bilingual BM/EN.

PLAY

You are Datuk Nadia at NADMA. 8 calls in 7 minutes. 4 gauges respond to every choice — lives, assets, trust, time.

COACH

2-stage Google ADK agent streams reasoning live. Assessor queries 9 MCP tools. Recommender places a ghost drone on the map.

WATCH AI

5-stage autonomous pipeline — Assess, Strategise, Dispatch, Analyse, Brief — drives the whole simulation hands-free.

One engine · three lenses.

Deterministic simulation at the core. Gemini 2.5 Pro at the boundary — not in the loop. Google ADK + MCP power the agentic modes. Real MetMalaysia feed.

The 3D map is the engine.

20×20 grid. Real kampung from Kelantan and Johor (via locality.py). A* pathfinding for drones. 9 MCP tools discovered at runtime via tools/list_changed. Reproducible. Off-loop LLM.

Two agents · same engine.

COACH is the 2-stage helper. WATCH AI is the 5-stage autonomous commander. Both discover MCP tools at runtime — no hard-coded drone IDs — and fail gracefully when degraded.
